This is the complete 2026 tier list for every common and rare card in Slay the Spire 2. We cover the best cards for each character, the cards that win runs, and the underdrafted cards that most players skip.

This is a companion guide to our [Slay the Spire 2 starter builds article](/roguelikes/slay-the-spire-2-starter-builds/): that article covers the first-3-cards strategy, this article covers the deeper tier rankings for the entire card pool.

## Tier ranking system

We use the same five-tier system as our weapon tier lists:

- **S tier** - the cards that win runs on their own
- **A tier** - strong cards that win runs with the right support
- **B tier** - solid cards that fit specific builds
- **C tier** - niche cards that only work in specific builds
- **D tier** - avoid if you have a choice

Tiers reflect the average power of a card across all runs in which it is picked up. A C-tier card in the right build can outperform an S-tier card in the wrong one.

## S tier: the run-winning cards

These cards can carry a run on their own. Pick them up whenever you see them.

### Ironclad S tier

- **Limit Break** (rare) - doubles your strength. Pairs with Inflame and Spot Weakness for infinite scaling. The single most impactful Ironclad rare.
- **Demon Form** (rare) - gain 2 strength at the start of every turn. The strongest long-term scaling card in the game.
- **Barricade** (rare) - block does not reset at the start of your turn. Pairs with Entrench for unbeatable defense.
- **Reaper** (rare) - deals 4 damage to all enemies and heals you for the damage dealt. The only single-target healing in the Ironclad pool.

### Silent S tier

- **Catalyst** (rare) - double the poison on a target. The strongest burst damage card in the game.
- **Burst** (rare) - play the next power 3 times. Pairs with Noxious Fumes for unstoppable poison application.
- **Malaise** (rare) - applies 2 weak and 2 frail to an enemy. The single strongest debuff in the game.
- **Wraith Form** (rare) - gain 2 intangible each turn (next attack deals 0 damage). Pairs with intangible stacking for free wins.

### Defect S tier

- **Echo Form** (rare) - your first card each turn is played twice. The strongest single card in the game.
- **Creative AI** (rare) - add a random power to your hand every turn. The most powerful scaling card in the game.
- **Devotion** (rare) - gain 2 strength each turn. Pairs with strength orbs for unbeatable scaling.

## A tier: strong cards for specific builds

These cards do not win runs on their own, but they enable specific builds that win runs. Pick them up if they fit your draft.

### Ironclad A tier

- **Inflame** (basic) - gain 2 strength. Cheap, repeatable, scales with energy.
- **Spot Weakness** (common) - gain 3 strength when you play an attack this turn.
- **Pommel Strike** (common) - 9 damage and draw 1 card. Strong draw engine.
- **Anger** (common) - 6 damage and add a copy to your discard. Card draw plus damage.
- **True Grit** (common) - 8 block, exhaust a card. Strong defensive card cycle.
- **Entrench** (uncommon) - double your block. Pairs with Barricade.

### Silent A tier

- **Deadly Poison** (common) - 5 poison. Cheap poison application.
- **Noxious Fumes** (common) - 2 poison to all enemies each turn. Strong defensive option.
- **Bouncing Flask** (common) - random enemy 3 poison 3 times. Strong AoE.
- **Heel Hook** (common) - 8 damage and draw 1. Strong draw engine.
- **Dodge and Roll** (common) - 4 block, draw 1. Defensive card draw.
- **Accuracy** (common) - shivs deal 4 more damage. Enables shiv archetype.

### Defect A tier

- **Defragment** (common) - gain 1 focus. The most important Defect card.
- **Loop** (common) - draw 1 when you channel an orb. Strong draw engine.
- **Capacitor** (common) - gain 2 orb slots. Strong power spike.
- **Zap** (basic) - channel a Lightning orb. Cheap channel.
- **Cold Snap** (common) - channel a Frost orb. Free block.

## S tier colorless cards: always relevant

- **Apotheosis** (rare) - upgrade all cards in your hand. The single strongest colorless rare. Pick it up every time.
- **Hand of Greed** (common) - gain gold equal to damage dealt. Strong economic card.
- **Bandage Up** (uncommon) - heal 4 HP. Strong defensive common.
- **Dark Shackles** (uncommon) - apply 1 weak to an enemy each turn. Strong debuff.

## B tier: solid cards that fit builds

These cards are strong but only work in specific builds. Pick them up if they fit your current direction.

### Ironclad B tier

- **Iron Wave** (common) - 5 damage, 5 block for 1 energy. The single most efficient block card.
- **Shrug It Off** (common) - 8 block, draw 1. Strong defensive card draw.
- **Battle Trance** (common) - draw 3, cost 1 energy but cannot play more cards this turn. Powerful in zero-cost decks.
- **Headbutt** (common) - 6 damage, place a card from discard on top of draw. Strong card cycle.
- **Feel No Pain** (uncommon) - gain 2 block whenever you exhaust a card. Strong in exhaust decks.

### Silent B tier

- **Acrobatics** (common) - draw 3, discard 1. Strong draw engine.
- **Backstab** (common) - 11 damage if from discard pile. One-shot burst.
- **Cloak and Dagger** (common) - 1 block, add a shiv to hand. Shiv enabler.
- **Blade Dance** (common) - add 3 shivs to hand. Shiv enabler.
- **Piercing Wail** (common) - all enemies lose 4 strength. Strong debuff.

### Defect B tier

- **Dualcast** (common) - evoke your next orb twice. The strongest common Defect card.
- **Compile Driver** (common) - draw 1 for each unique orb channel. Strong in multi-orb decks.
- **Claw** (common) - deal 3 damage, permanently gain 2 strength. Claw archetype enabler.
- **Steam** (uncommon) - gain 1 energy and add a Steam power. Strong energy engine.
- **Force Field** (common) - gain 4 block per orb in your slots. Strong defensive card.

## C tier: niche cards

These cards only work in specific builds. Pick them up if you are running a dedicated build around them.

### Ironclad C tier

- **Juggernaut** (uncommon) - 5 damage, deal 5 damage whenever you gain block. Only works in heavy-block decks.
- **Corruption** (rare) - all skills cost 0 and exhaust. Strong in exhaust decks, weak elsewhere.
- **Exhume** (rare) - return an exhaust card from discard to hand. Strong with specific exhaust cards.
- **Impervious** (uncommon) - 30 block for 2 energy. Strong in panic, weak in normal turns.

### Silent C tier

- **Bullet Time** (rare) - draw 3 cards, costs 0 next turn. Strong in multi-attack decks.
- **Phantasmal Killer** (rare) - next attack deals double damage. Strong burst, weak alone.
- **Storm of Steel** (rare) - add a shiv for each card in your hand. Strong in shiv archetype only.

### Defect C tier

- **Hyperbeam** (rare) - 12 damage, lose 1 focus. Strong opening, weak late.
- **Meteor Strike** (rare) - 24 damage. Strong burst, weak without setup.
- **Thunder Strike** (uncommon) - 5 damage per Lightning orb. Strong in Lightning orb decks.

## D tier: avoid

These cards are outclassed by other options in the current patch. If you see them in a run, take a different card if one is offered.

### Ironclad D tier

- **Clothesline** (common) - 12 damage, 2 weak. Outclassed by Pommel Strike and Anger.
- **Cleave** (common) - 8 damage to all enemies. Outclassed by Bash and Fiend Fire.

### Silent D tier

- **Neutralize** (common) - 3 damage, 2 weak. Outclassed by Heel Hook and Bouncing Flask.
- **Survivor** (common) - 8 block, discard 1. Outclassed by Dodge and Roll.

### Defect D tier

- **Beam Cell** (common) - 4 damage, 1 plasma. Outclassed by Zap and Cold Snap.
- **Leap** (common) - 9 block, draw 2. Outclassed by Compile Driver and Loop.

## How to use this tier list

The tier list is a starting point, not a rule. The most important thing is to find cards that fit your current draft. If you are building a strength deck for the Ironclad, an S-tier poison card for the Silent is not worth picking up.

The key principle: pick cards that win runs, not cards that are theoretically strong in isolation. A 5-card deck of B-tier cards that work together will outperform a 20-card deck of mixed-tier cards that have no plan.
